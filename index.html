Data Mesh for Business, Tech and Product Leaders - November 26th in Cologne

Data Mesh is a business goals driven approach to thinking about data. In a world of complex data architectures - data mesh, when done right, can pave the way to fast and powerful data driven outcomes. In this hands-on workshop, you will learn the essentials of data mesh and a hands on guide required to evaluate, advocate for, and implement a data mesh transition.
